HCI In AI-XR In Education

Games and Virtual Reality in Learning Sciences

  • Research Topic: Enhancing STEM Learning Through Immersive VR Environments

  • To: Explore how VR games can be tailored to enhance understanding and retention of STEM knowledge, aligning with the program's interest in designing effective digital environments for learning.

  • Methodology: Design a prototype VR game focusing on specific STEM topics. Conduct a quasi-experimental study where one group of students learns through traditional means while another uses the VR game. Employ mixed methods: quantitative data (e.g., pre-post tests) to gauge learning outcomes and qualitative data (e.g., interviews) to understand students' experiences. Analyze results to discern the effectiveness and areas of improvement for VR in STEM education.

    • Human-AI Interaction in Education

  • Research Topic: Minimizing AI Biases in Off-Campus Education

  • To: Integrate human-computer interaction and AI to offer a personalized, bias-free learning experience, especially in STEM fields.

  • Methodology: Design an AI-based educational tool that incorporates principles from human-computer interaction to tailor content delivery. Pilot the tool in off-campus settings, like online courses or digital workshops. Gather data on student engagement, understanding, and feedback on the AI tool's personalization features. Analyze data to determine if biases have been mitigated and the overall effectiveness of the AI-enhanced learning platform.
